blanket forts

my name is mike esposito. i have been making experimental electronic music for 20 years now. you might even call it IDM. currently i make it under the name blanket forts. my current hardware setup is two nord modular synthesizers, an octatrack sampler, a tiny mixer and a bunch of cables. i record everything live to audacity, except when i don't. when i don't, the track is almost always a software project in logic pro 9, or a hyprid hardware/logic project. i play live gigs with just the hardware; it just feels weird to me to pull a laptop on stage and there isn't much to do to be honest. on the other hand, i use a laptop exclusively for djing because i can get access to my entire music library and it works out well. my dj style has been self-defined as "manic illbient" since 2001. when i dj i use capital letters and it's called DJ Blanketforts. right now i dj for lumpen radio in chicago with a once-monthly show that i prep and send to them. but enough chatter; go on and click on a link below! oh, and math is definitely not music. be forewarned.
